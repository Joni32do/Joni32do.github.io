My major in Simulation Technology (SimTech) at the University of Stuttgart is an interdisciplinary program that combines elements of

* computer science
* mathematics
* engineering
* natural sciences
  
to equip students with the skills needed to model, simulate, and analyze complex systems. For the curious readers I dedicate more blog posts to dive deeper into specific topics. It will summarize core concepts and link to further resources.
